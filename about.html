About Design Dwell

MISSION

Design Dwell is your regional design + build resource to explore luxury interior and exterior design, beautifully built homes, furnishings, artwork, alluring landscapes, home goods and services and prestigious properties. You'll find a wide array of upscale furnishings, artwork, photos of rooms created by professional interior designers and homes built by our renowned architects and home builders. We offer a place where you can choose design + build professionals, shop and save your favorite design ideas.

In addition to our online resource platform we print a lookbook previewing our talented design + build community and then send it to the area's most affluent homeowners and The Trade with an invitation to join our exclusive community.

HISTORY

CEO Cheryl Hullin founded Design Digest in 1996, a popular and successful high-end interior design guide serving Tampa Bay and Sarasota. This exclusive monthly publication featured the best of the region's interior and exterior design businesses, winning multiple awards for design and content year after year. It quickly became the area’s premier “go-to” source for luxury home-goods, architecture, and design and won the praise of its advertising businesses because, as they'll tell you in their own words, "It simply worked!"

The transition to Design Dwell was simply a natural succession.
